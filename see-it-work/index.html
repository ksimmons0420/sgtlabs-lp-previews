What everyone quietly wants to know

Honestly — how complicated is setup?

Watch the Stopwatch Setup video and judge for yourself. Because we integrate with Google Admin, Active Directory and your SIS, most of the “hard part” is data you already have. Most districts are live the same week.

What happens every new term?

Your SIS drives it. New schedules and rosters sync through the integration, so term changes flow in automatically instead of becoming a manual project.

Does it cover a mixed device fleet?

Yes — Chromebooks, Windows, Mac, iPad, virtual desktops, even networked printers, all from one console.

Who do we call when something breaks?

A US-based human in Wisconsin, with a 45-minute average to both respond and resolve. Not a queue, not a bot, not a pasted help-doc.

What about student data?

In 25 years we have never viewed, sold, stored, or shared student data. We sign the strictest state Data Privacy Agreements and have no ad-tech parent company.
