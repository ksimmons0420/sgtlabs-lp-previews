Before you renew

Switching questions, answered straight

Is the price really all-in?

Yes. Filtering, geolocation, behavior analytics and classroom control are one price. Updates and new features are included at no charge — there is no separate module to add later.

How painful is switching mid-year?

Less than you think. We integrate with Google Admin Console, Active Directory and your SIS, so rostering carries over. Most districts are live within the same week, with a named specialist on the phone the whole way.

Will it work across all our devices?

Chromebooks, Windows, Mac, iPad, virtual desktops — and yes, even networked printers. If it’s in your building, we most likely cover it.

What happens to student data?

In 25 years we have never viewed, sold, stored, or shared student data. We sign the most restrictive state Data Privacy Agreements and have no ad-tech parent company.

Are you CIPA-ready?

Yes. AristotleK12 covers the filtering districts are federally required to have — and a good deal more the bare-minimum firewall never will.
